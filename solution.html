The Solution!

  • Speak to Your Representatives
    Tell your state representatives that we need Net Neutrality! Representatives can draft bills that would make Net Neutrality a law or even an ammendment. Here is a list of representatives by state.
  • Join the Fight
    Use websites like Save The Internet or Battle for the Net to sign petitions, find events, make calls, or donate. Another great place to fight for Net Neutrality is Public Knowledge. Also use social media! Use #NetNeutrality on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
    #NetNeutrality Tweets
  • Protest
    This could be just participating in July 12's protest. It could be boycotting the internet. It could be going with Public Knowledge or some other group and protesting in front of your city's capital building or the white house. For more info about the July 12th: Save the Internet Day go here
