What is Net Neutrality?

Net Neutrality forces ISPs to charge the same price for all types of content. This means they cannot create packages of websites. Also, they cannot make some websites faster than others. In one sentence, Net Neutrality declares all bits as equal. Now if you don't what a bit is, then you should think of it as 1 or a 0. A bit is one binary character. Binary is essentially what our computers turn everything into to read. Binary is the language computers speak.
